# Sources: Euclid strong lensing

## Euclid Collaboration: Walmsley et al. 2025 — Discovery Engine A

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Q1-SP048`
**Reference:** A&A, Euclid Q1 special issue; system overview and lens catalogue
**Concepts:** [[lens-finding]], [[q1-dr1-releases]]

**Supports:**
- The Q1 Strong Lensing Discovery Engine combined ML models, citizen science and
  expert grading over the ~63 deg² Q1 field.
- ~500 galaxy-galaxy candidates including ~250 grade A from Q1.

**Use when:** citing the Q1 lens catalogue or the discovery-system architecture.
**Do not use for:** DR1-scale numbers (in-prep DR1 papers) or spectroscopic confirmation.

## Euclid Collaboration: Rojas et al. 2025 — Discovery Engine B

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Q1-SP052`
**Reference:** A&A, Euclid Q1 special issue
**Concepts:** [[lens-finding]], [[selection-effects]]

**Supports:**
- Early strong-lens candidates from targeted visual inspection of high
  velocity-dispersion galaxies in Q1.

**Use when:** citing the visual-inspection leg of the Q1 search or
velocity-dispersion preselection.

## Euclid Collaboration: Lines et al. 2025 — Discovery Engine C

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Q1-SP053`
**Reference:** A&A, Euclid Q1 special issue
**Concepts:** [[lens-finding]], [[deep-learning-lensing]]

**Supports:**
- Machine-learning lens finding in Q1 (CNN-based search).

**Use when:** citing the ML leg of the Q1 search. A follow-up on advancing ML
detection with real Euclid training data is `Q1-SP085` (Lines et al. 2025).

## Euclid Collaboration: Li et al. 2025 — Discovery Engine D

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Q1-SP054`
**Reference:** A&A, Euclid Q1 special issue
**Concepts:** [[lens-finding]]

**Supports:**
- Double-source-plane lens candidates from Q1 — the rare compound systems most
  valuable for cosmography.

## Euclid Collaboration: Holloway et al. 2025 — Discovery Engine E

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Q1-SP059`
**Reference:** A&A, Euclid Q1 special issue
**Concepts:** [[lens-finding]], [[selection-effects]]

**Supports:**
- Ensemble classification of strong lens candidates — combining rankings from
  multiple independent classifiers into calibrated grades.

## Euclid Collaboration: Ecker et al. 2026 — Discovery Engine F

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Q1-Ecker`
**Reference:** A&A, Euclid Q1 special issue
**Concepts:** [[lens-finding]], [[selection-effects]]

**Supports:**
- Bright and low-redshift strong lenses systematically missed by the main SLDE
  selection (Gaia-counterpart bias); targeted recovery search.

## Euclid Collaboration: Vincken et al. 2026 — AstroVink

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Q1-Vincken`
**Reference:** A&A
**Concepts:** [[deep-learning-lensing]], [[lens-finding]]

**Supports:**
- Vision-transformer (ViT, DINOv2-based) lens finding on Euclid imaging.

## Acevedo Barroso et al. 2025 — ERO lens search experiment

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `AcevedoBarroso24`
**Reference:** A&A; doi:10.1051/0004-6361/202451868
**Concepts:** [[lens-finding]]

**Supports:**
- The Early Release Observations lens search — the first Euclid-data lens-finding
  exercise, pre-Q1.

## O'Riordan et al. 2025 — NGC 6505 Einstein ring

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `ORiordan25`
**Reference:** A&A
**Concepts:** [[einstein-radius]], [[lens-finding]]

**Supports:**
- Discovery and modeling of a complete Einstein ring in the nearby galaxy
  NGC 6505 — an emblematic early Euclid strong-lensing result.

## In-prep DR1 papers (preliminary — no bib entries yet)

The DR1 strong-lensing series is collaboration-internal at the time of writing:
Lines et al. 2026 (DR1 lens catalogue, >10,000 candidates over ~2000 deg²),
Holloway et al. 2026 (grading calibration), Nightingale et al. 2026 (standardised
lensing-model fits — produced with `euclid_strong_lens_modeling_pipeline`),
Nersesian et al. 2026 (lens photo-zs), Vincken et al. 2026a (DR1 ML pipeline),
Fogliardi et al. 2026 ([[euclid-dr1-image-processing|image-processing ablation]]).
Treat all quoted numbers as preliminary; add bibliography entries only once public
metadata exists.
