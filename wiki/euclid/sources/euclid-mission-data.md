# Sources: Euclid mission, instruments and data

## Euclid Collaboration: Mellier et al. 2025 — Mission overview

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `EuclidSkyOverview`
**Reference:** A&A; doi:10.1051/0004-6361/202450810
**Concepts:** [[euclid-mission]], [[euclid-wide-survey]]

**Supports:**
- Mission design, survey strategy, expected performance; the 14,000 deg² Wide
  Survey and ~1.5 billion galaxies.

**Use when:** the single canonical citation for "Euclid".

## Boucaud et al. 2016 — Convolution kernels

**Canonical BibTeX key:** `Boucaud2016`
**Reference:** A&A 596, A63; arXiv:1609.02006; doi:10.1051/0004-6361/201629080
**Concepts:** [[psf-homogenisation]]

**Supports:**
- Wiener-filter kernel construction (with tunable regularisation) for matching a
  high-resolution PSF to a lower-resolution one — the algorithm MER photometry
  uses.
