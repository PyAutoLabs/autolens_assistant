# Sources: GR tests and cosmology

## Collett 2018 — γ_PPN with ESO325-G004

**File:** `Strong_Lens/Collett2018GR.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[gr-tests]]
**Summary:** Precision extragalactic test of the post-Newtonian parameter
`gamma` using the nearby lens ESO325-G004. By combining the lensing mass
scale with stellar-dynamical information, the paper tests whether the
space-curvature response per unit mass is consistent with General
Relativity and finds agreement at roughly the 10 per cent level.

## Melo 2023 — probing GR

**File:** `Strong_Lens/Melo2023ProbeGR.pdf`
**Concepts:** [[gr-tests]]
**Summary:** Extends galaxy-scale GR tests beyond the local ESO325-G004
case by combining ALMA, HST, and VLT/MUSE data for the SDP.81 lens galaxy
at `z = 0.299`. The inferred `eta_PPN` is consistent with GR once mass
model, kinematic, and cosmological systematics are included, showing that
this style of lensing-plus-dynamics test can be pushed to higher redshift.
