## Chu 2013 — multipoles

**Reference:** Chu 2013 — multipoles
**Concepts:** [[multipoles]]

## Cohen 2024 — multipoles

**Reference:** Cohen 2024 — multipoles
**Concepts:** [[multipoles]], [[dark-matter-substructure]]
**Summary:** Demonstrates that ignoring m=4 multipoles produces
false subhalo detections; must be marginalised over in modern
analyses.

## Stacey 2024 — ALMA multipoles

**Reference:** Stacey 2024 — ALMA multipoles
**Concepts:** [[multipoles]], [[interferometric-lensing]]
**Summary:** Shows that ALMA-resolved lenses can exhibit clear m=4
angular structure, and that including these multipoles materially
improves the fit and reduces false substructure inferences.
