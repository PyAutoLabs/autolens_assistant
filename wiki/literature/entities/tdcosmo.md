# TDCOSMO

## What it is

Successor to [[h0licow|H0liCOW]]. TDCOSMO ("Time-Delay COSMOgraphy")
extended the lensed-quasar sample, added new monitoring, and — crucially
— relaxed the power-law assumption that H0liCOW had relied on.

## Key result

TDCOSMO IV (Birrer 2020) allowed an internal mass-sheet transformation
parameterised by a free λ_int, yielding H0 ~ 67–74 km/s/Mpc with
substantially broader uncertainty (~8% rather than ~2%). Combining with
external velocity-dispersion data from SLACS partially closed the
[[mass-sheet-degeneracy]] and tightened the posterior.

## Why it matters

The contrast between [[h0licow]] (mass model fixed, ~2% H0) and TDCOSMO
IV (mass model relaxed, ~8% H0) became the canonical illustration that
strong-lensing H0 is mass-model-systematic-limited, not data-limited.
