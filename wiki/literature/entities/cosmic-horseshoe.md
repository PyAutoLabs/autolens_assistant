# The Cosmic Horseshoe

## What it is

A near-perfect Einstein ring with θ_E ~ 5″, formed by a luminous red
galaxy at z = 0.444 lensing a star-forming galaxy at z = 2.379. One of
the largest known galaxy-scale Einstein rings. Used as a high-S/N
testbed for source reconstruction (Dye 2009) and for source-plane
high-z science (James 2018 UV).
