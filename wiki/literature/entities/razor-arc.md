# The Razor Arc

## What it is

A long, thin lensed arc geometry close to a critical curve, providing
exceptional sensitivity to small perturbers (SMBHs, subhaloes) of the
mass model. Banik 2019 used the razor-arc configuration to forecast SMBH
mass limits.
