# BELLS / BELLS-GALLERY

## What it is

BELLS — BOSS Emission-Line Lens Survey (Brownstein, Bolton et al.).
Spectroscopic lens sample selected from SDSS-III/BOSS galaxies (higher
redshift than [[slacs]]). BELLS-GALLERY (Shu 2016) is the Lyman-α-emitter
subsample with sources at z_s ~ 2–3.

## Key facts

- Lens redshifts z_l ~ 0.3–0.7 (higher than SLACS).
- BELLS-GALLERY sources are LAEs, often compact and bright.
- Used in [[dark-matter-substructure|substructure detection]] work
  (Ritondale 2018) and to access higher-z lensing kinematics.

## Founding citation

**Brownstein et al. 2012** — "The BOSS Emission-Line Lens Survey (BELLS).
I." (arXiv:1112.3683, ApJ 744, 41). Extends the SLACS methodology to
BOSS spectra, defining the higher-redshift BELLS sample. Full read-up
in [[sources-lens-surveys]].
