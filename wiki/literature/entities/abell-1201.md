# Abell 1201

## What it is

A nearby (z ~ 0.17) galaxy cluster whose BCG produces a strong lens with
a counter-image — the geometry uniquely sensitive to a central
[[smbh-from-lensing|SMBH]]. Nightingale 2023 used PyAutoLens to place
the tightest upper limit on a galactic SMBH (M_BH ≲ 3 × 10¹⁰ M_⊙)
in this system, with sensitivity reaching ultramassive BH territory.

## Key facts

- Cluster discovery: Edge 2003 X-ray; lens characterised by Smith 2017.
- The Nightingale 2023 analysis is the principal PyAutoLens SMBH paper.
- Demonstrates the central-image methodology of [[smbh-from-lensing]].
