# SL2S — Strong Lensing Legacy Survey

## What it is

CFHTLS-derived sample of strong galaxy-galaxy lenses found by the
**Ringfinder** algorithm (Gavazzi 2014) — looking for blue residuals
around red ellipticals after PSF subtraction. Bridges
[[slacs|SLACS]]-style spectroscopic samples to fully photometric finders.

## Key facts

- z_l ~ 0.3–0.8, higher than SLACS.
- Imaging-based selection → different selection function from SLACS.
- Used to study the **evolution** of mass slope and DM fraction with
  redshift.
