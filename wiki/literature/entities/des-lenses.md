# DES lens samples

## What it is

The Dark Energy Survey produced large catalogues of strong-lens
candidates via CNN classification (Jacobs 2019, Rojas 2022). The AGEL
(ANU Gemini Lens) spectroscopic confirmation programme (Tran 2022)
follows up candidates.

## Key facts

- DES area ~ 5000 deg² → thousands of candidates.
- Shallower than HSC; selects brighter lenses.
- CNN finders trained on simulations; selection function inherits the
  training-set assumptions.
