# H0liCOW

## What it is

H0LiCOW = "H0 Lenses in COSMOGRAIL's Wellspring". A multi-institutional
collaboration that measured H0 from time-delay cosmography in 6 lensed
quasars (Wong 2019). Final lensing-only result H0 = 73.3 ± 1.7 km/s/Mpc,
consistent with the local distance ladder, ~3σ tension with Planck under
ΛCDM.

## Methodology

- Power-law + external shear lens models with [[external-convergence-shear|κ_ext
  priors]] from LOS counting.
- Stellar velocity dispersion to break the
  [[mass-sheet-degeneracy]] under the power-law assumption.
- COSMOGRAIL time-delay measurements (years of monitoring).

## Successor

The TDCOSMO collaboration ([[tdcosmo]]) extended H0liCOW with relaxed
mass-model assumptions, yielding broader uncertainty but consistent with
both Planck and the distance ladder. The methodology shift was central
to the post-2020 [[hubble-tension|Hubble-tension]] story.

## Founding citation

**Suyu et al. 2017** — "H0LiCOW I." (arXiv:1607.00017, MNRAS 468, 2590).
