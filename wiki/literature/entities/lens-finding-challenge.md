# The Strong-Lens Finding Challenge

## What it is

Community blind challenge (Metcalf 2018) that benchmarked automated
lens-finding algorithms — visual, classical-machine-learning, and
deep-learning — on a common simulated dataset. Established CNN-based
finders as the standard.
