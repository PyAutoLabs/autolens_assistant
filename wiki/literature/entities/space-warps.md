# Space Warps

## What it is

Citizen-science lens-finding project on the Zooniverse platform. Users
classify imaging cutouts as lens / not-lens; results provide labelled
training data for ML pipelines and discover rare morphologies missed by
automated finders. The "Spaghetti" paper (2017) describes the discovery
of unusual lensed morphologies.
